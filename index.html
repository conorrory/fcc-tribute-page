The person that saved a billion lives

The above picture, does not show DR. Norman Borlaug, instead it is a placeholder image to set the mood.

  • 1914 - Born.
  • 1933 - Attends University of Minnesota.
  • 1935 - Leaves studies to work, observes people starving which leaves an impact.
  • 1937 - Finishes University, joins US forest service.
  • 1938 - Get's married, laid off, goes back to study.
  • 1941 - Attempts to enlist, denied and sent to work on science.
  • 1942 - Ph.D. in Genetics and Plant Pathology.
  • 1944 - Leaves Dupont, Leaves Wife, Goes to Mexico to Grow plants.
  • 1945 - Doubles Wheat Yields.
  • 1953 - Cross breeds wheat.
  • 1962 - Visits India, gives wheat.
  • 1970 - Nobel peace prize
  • 1983 - Helps 7 african countries.
  • 1984 - becomes a professor at Texas A&M University
  • 2005 - States we will need to double world food supply by 2050, champions GM products...
  • 2009 - dies age 95.
